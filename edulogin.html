Question Paper in Seconds

Preparing an effective question paper from scratch is a complex and a time-consuming task. Various examinations are conducted in a year and educators need to create various question papers as per the requirements. It is very difficult to cover all features of the course outcomes based on the updating course outline. Therefore, the quality of the question paper relies entirely on an individual educator’s experience and proficiency. Hence there appears a requirement to have a system which will automatically create the question paper from the scheme of evaluation given by the educators within a few seconds. The proposed user-friendly system helps educators to save time in preparing question papers for the examination. The Automatic Question Paper Generation System delivers diverse benefits to the user when compared to the traditional system like questions associated with each unit can be uploaded based on the weightage, the database can be updated based on the changing syllabus etc… The goal of our project is to develop a prototype of an automatic question paper generator, later which can be iterated as a full-fledged solution.
